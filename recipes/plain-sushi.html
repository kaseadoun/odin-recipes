Plain Sushi Recipe

459 grains of rice individually placed in accordance to the Sushi Masters Chefs Guild (S.M.C.G.), hand picked and each weighed within a tolerance of 1g.

Ingredients

  • 459 Grains of Rice
  • A Sushi Masters Chefs Guild (S.M.C.G.) Certificate
  • A Dollop of Wasabi (Optional - Save it for later, but save some for later you might need it.)
  • Scale (Precise to 0.01 metric grams)
  • 1 "The Verge" Certified Zip-Tie

Steps

  1. Ensure your S.M.C.G. certificate is explicitly visible to assert your credibility to your guests.
  2. Weigh each grain to ensure the weight of the grain is within a tolerance of 1 metric gram (Also known as 0.001 tonnes).
  3. Use your "The Verge" Certified Zip-Tie to carefully bond each grain.
  4. Bon Appetite
